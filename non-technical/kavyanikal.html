The date of the auditions will be notified soon on the official page of 'TECHNOVATION 2K18'.
 The participants from outside colleges, who are not able to give auditions will have to send their content on the official page of ‘TECHNOVATION 2K18’ via text message onwards 2nd April.
 All the participants of I.E.R.T. are hereby informed that there will be three rounds for the event:
Round 1:
 All the candidates will face an audition round on 2nd April(probably) before the various co-ordinators.
 Participants should bring their self-written poetries for the audition.
 All participants will be given 4-5 minutes only at the audition time.
 The content should be proper, i.e. it will not hurt one's feeling, religion, faith etc.
 Students will be selected on behalf of the content, tone, and the presentation skillS.
 The selected candidates will be notified on the official page and surpass for the subsequent round.
Round 2:
 All the selected aspirants from round 1 will gather on a pre-decided venue on a given date (probably 5th April).
 All participants will be given a slip, with a mentioned topic on it. All candidates will write a poem, ghazal, Shayari etc. as per their interest regarding the given topic.
 They will also provided sufficient time for it.
 After finishing the task, all participants should submit their pages to the co-ordinators.
 The result for this round will be declared & updated on the official page in few hours.
Round 3:
 All selected candidates in round 2 should be present in seminar hall on 8th April for the final round at the given time.
 All participants are allowed to perform last time on the mic dice for max. 8 minutes.
 This is the open round. Candidates can recite their poetries on their own pleasant topics.
 Jury will be present there for the final judgment.
 Finally, the top 3 position candidates will be selected and notified after hour.
