1. The dimensions of the bot should be in the range of (15 - 30)cm x (15 - 30)cmx(5 - 15)cm (l x b x h).

2. The weight of the bot should not exceed 3 kg. This does not include the weight of all external control devices.

3. The bot can be manual or semi-autonomous.

4. The speed of the motors used must not exceed 200 RPM.

5. 15 V and 5 amp(max), DC supply will be allowed to the teams during the event. AC supply is not allowed to the bot.Team Should bring their own AC to DC adapters.

6. In case of wireless bot:Teams can use 3 cell lithium polymer or lithium ion batteries for power supply(max 24 W).

7. Use of an IC engine in any form is not allowed.
8. Arms and clamps can be used in the bot.

1. A team will be disqualified if it is not present with its bot on the allotted time.

2. The team will be disqualified if its bot fails to fulfill the given bot specifications.

3. The team will be disqualified if its bot is found to be damaging the arena.

4. The bot will be checked for safety of the the arena, and would be disqualified if found unsafe.

5. Flying of bot using air foil, helium balloons, etc. is not allowed.

6. The organizers reserve the right to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit.

7. The decision of organizers will be final and binding on all.

8. Teams misbehaving with any of organizers will be disqualified and in unexpected cases decision of Event Head will be final.

1. A team can consist of maximum of five members.

2. Students from different educational institutes can form a team.

3. The students have to arrive with a valid identity card of their respective educational institution.
