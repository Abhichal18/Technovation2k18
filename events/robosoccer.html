RoboSoccer

  • Coordinators

In the Robosoccer event ,two robots competes against each other .They must look for a ball trying to score in a special field built in a way that resembles the actual field for human soccer .

 A team can have a maximum number of 5 members.

 A team is allowed to play with only one robot.

 In case the robot moves out of the arena, the robot will be placed at the previous point.

 Touching the robot during the game will lead to negative points.

 In case of wired robot the wire should remain slack and lifted throughout the race. It should not disturb the arena or the participants. If so then the team will be disqualified.

 In case of wireless robot, it should contain its own power

 Unfair game may lead to disqualification of the team.

 The robot should not damage the field. Damaging/harming the arena may lead to disqualification.

 Decision of the event coordinator shall be treated as final and binding on all and cannot be contested.


 The event will be timed and the side scoring the maximum goals within the playtime shall be declared the winner.

 The robot found pushing or damaging others shall be disqualified.


1. The robot must easily fit into a box of 30cm x 30cm x20cm. (lxbxh)

2. Maximum voltage in the circuit should not exceed 15V at any time.

3. Current should not exceed 5 Amperes for both wireless and wired.

4. The RPM of motors used to make the robots should not exceed 200.

5. Weight of Bot must not exceed 4 kg.
