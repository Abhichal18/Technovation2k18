“Imagination is the beginning of creation.”

Connection is the energy that exists between two components of the universe .The whole World hangs on the itinerary of cosmos. It is the ultimatum for the hertz of life. It's high time to use all those equations and formulas and mugged up paragraphs. Design your own structures and give it life by making it a real model!
The event argues you to apply your creativity and engineer brains and innovate a Popsicle bridge that takes maximum load being lightest of all.

OBJECTIVE: To design the most stable and innovative structure of bridge model that can bear maximum load being lightest of all.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:
1-Every team should consist of at least 3 and at most 5 members.
2-time allotted -3 hours.

MATERIALS:
1-Popsicle (ice cream sticks) and glue (fevicol only).
2-These materials shall be provided at the event site by the event coordinators.
3-No piece of any construction material shall be allowed with the participants during the event.
4-Cloth drying clips can be brought by participants to clutch the structure during drying.
5-glue cannot be used on the outer surface.

DESIGNING CONSTRAINTS:
1-Height of bridge should not exceed 15cm.
2-width should not exceed 15 cm.
3-clear span = 50cm between the supports.
4-The deck should be smooth enough to pass a tennis ball through it.

FIRST STAGE:
Interested participants are requested to submit their “team name” along with their team member details during the time of registration.


SECOND STAGE:
At the event site,the participants shall be provided with 200 ice cream sticks and 2 tubes of fevicol .
Every team shall be provided a separate drawing table.The total allotted time will be three hours for construction.the model will be kept as it is and dried overnight.
Testing is done on the next day.
