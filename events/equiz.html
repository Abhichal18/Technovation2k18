“ TALENT WINS GAMES , BUT TEAMWORK AND INTELLIGENCE WINS CHAMPIONSHIPS.”

You can involve yourselves in electronics, computers, puzzles, Their is a lot of creativity and brain working.
Then here you have a chance to show your skills.

This quiz is to check your basic knowledge related to Electronics and Electrical.

Student can participate individually or in pair. There will be 2 levels in this event.

LEVEL 1:Qualifier Round(60 min)
Rule: 45 MCQs to be solved in 60 min based on fundamental concept of Electronics and Electrical. Shortlisted students will be promoted to 2nd round.

LEVEL 2:Buzzer Round and Rapid Fire Round
Rule-Each team will have to give maximum answers in buzzer round and then a rapid fire round will be conducted in which each team will be asked 10 questions back to back.

The one with maximum right answers will be declared as winner.
