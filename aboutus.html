About Us

Institute of engineering & rural technology, Allahabad is one of the premier technical institute imparting high quality education & supportive research in the field of engineering. The institute is among one of the best engineering college in the contour of Allahabad for providing congenial environment for the holistic growth of the students. The institute is affiliated to Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam technical university, Lucknow.

OUR MOTTO: - “Work is Worship”.

Continuing the legacy we proudly bring TECHNOVATION’18, the annual technical fest of I.E.R.T-ALLAHABAD. TECHNOVATION’18 promises to present a plethora of events that would help students to enhance their skills in both technical as well as management fields. This technical extravaganza will provide ample of opportunities to students to showcase their talents in different fields of engineering and management along with non-technical events with hefty sum of prize money. Since the inception, TECHNOVATION always aimed at providing quality, competitive environment, as we believe it is the best way to enhance ones knowledge and also gives him a foretaste of competition that they will face in the future. SO GET READY FOR THE MAYHEM,GET READY FOR FUN AND GET READY FOR TECHNOVATION’18.
